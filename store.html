1887 GSA Morgan Silver Dollar

Grade: MS-65

Description: An 1887 Philadelphia Mint Morgan Silver Dollar from the GSA hoard. The GSA stands for the General Services Administration who foud a collection of millions of uncirculated Morgan silver dollars in various U.S. Treasury Vaults. These Morgans were then sold to the public between 1972 and 1980. The mintage of this coin is estimated at around 20,290,000, although it's unknown how many of them actually belong to the GSA Hoard.
